What we do
Our Services
Major components exchange

We ensure optimal turbine performance, through the replacement of components such as blades, bearings, generators, gearboxes, hubs, main bearings and yaw systems.

Pre-Installation and Preventive Maintenance

In the pre-installation phase, we provide complete support for concrete and steel towers, along with preventive programs including cleaning, lubrication, torquing operations, and UT/DT checks.

Staffing

We offer staffing solutions across Europe, providing qualified technical professionals across different phases of Installations and Major Components Upgrade.

750k+
Installation Hours

With over 750,000 workhours of installation experience, our vision is to accelerate the energy transition, making wind energy more accessible and competitive.

GE Units

In the last 5 years, the teams part of ETS participated to the installation of >550 GE units of which >300 large Cypress units, and executed around 1,000 major operations in the same period.

Safety First

People are at the core of our organization, so we are committed to continuously improving health and safety. Our leaders care about the teams, and they care for each other. We always support the STOP work when safety is at risk.

Quality and Innovation

With a strong focus on continuous innovation, we use LEAN methodologies, standard processes, and highly skilled teams to make every project safer, more efficient, and more sustainable.

Cost Productivity

Our execution strategy is based on driving down costs through process standardization, performance measurement, and a constant presence at Gemba.
